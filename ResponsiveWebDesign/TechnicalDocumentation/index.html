How to place the car on drive

Unlock the door

Sit Down
  1. Check that the Emergency Brak is up.
  2. The car is on parking.
  3. Seatbealt is on.
  4. You reach the breaks.
  5. You reach the accelerator.

Turn on ignition

Lower Emergency Break

Place Car On Drive
